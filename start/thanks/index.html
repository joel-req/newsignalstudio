Within 24 hours

You'll get a short email from Joel directly. It confirms the time, includes the kickoff prep doc with the questions we'll work through on the call, and gives you a way to reply with anything you'd like covered specifically.

Bring to the call

Five things, none of them slides.

  • The current funnel URL, or screenshots of the pages you're sending traffic to.
  • A monthly revenue ballpark. Round number is fine. We're calibrating, not auditing.
  • The KPI you actually care about: cost-per-qualified-lead, cost-per-customer, or both.
  • The ad spend ballpark you'd put behind the sprint. Floor is $1.5K to $3K.
  • Any prior agency experience worth flagging: what worked, what didn't, what you wouldn't repeat.

On the call

Roughly 45 minutes. Working format, not pitch format.

Joel walks the funnel and the offer with you. We talk through the math, agree on what qualified means in your specific case, and pressure-test the KPI you'd want on paper. By the end, you have a clear answer on whether a sprint can hit a number you'd actually pay for. If it can't, we say so.
