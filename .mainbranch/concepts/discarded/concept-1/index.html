§ 01

Three reasons this works.

  1. 01

    Skin in the game, on paper.

    Most agencies bill whether you grow or not. We bill on hit. You name the KPI. We agree to it in writing on the kickoff call. Miss it, you owe nothing past ad spend.

  2. 02

    Funnel, creative, and media buying. One sprint.

    No spreadsheet of recommendations. We rebuild the landing page, write the VSL, ship 5+ cold creatives and 5+ retargeting variations, and run the spend daily for 14 days.

  3. 03

    Two clean exits. No lock-in.

    After the sprint you either own the system (handoff, flat fee, we walk away) or keep us running it as a performance partnership. Structure is negotiated per client. No retainers without proof.

§ 02

What the 14 days actually look like.

Days 01–03

Build.

Landing page rebuilt. VSL script written or rewritten. Tracking instrumented. Creative concepts approved.

Days 04–07

Launch.

Cold-traffic campaign live with 5+ variations. Retargeting layered with 5+ variations. Spend opens against the agreed KPI.

Days 08–14

Optimize.

Daily media buying. Creative kills, scales, and refreshes. Funnel adjustments based on what the spend is actually saying.

Day 14

Decide.

Hit the KPI: pick Path A (handoff, flat fee) or Path B (performance partnership). Miss it: you keep everything we built. We invoice nothing.
