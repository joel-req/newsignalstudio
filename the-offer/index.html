Home/The offer

Risk reversal, on the record.

The terms in full. Nothing buried in a contract.

The sprint runs on ad spend you control. Our fee for the fourteen days is zero. Miss the KPI you signed off on, you keep every asset we built and you owe nothing. Hit it, you pick the path that fits your business. Two paths, both written down here.

During the sprint

What you pay. What we earn. What you keep.

The structure is built so a wrong call costs you a budget you would have spent anyway, not a check on top of it. The numbers below aren't fine print. They are the deal.

  • Sprint fee Zero. We don't charge for the fourteen days. You cover ad spend only.
  • Ad budget floor $1.5K to $3K minimum. Below that, the data is too noisy to read inside two weeks. If your budget is meaningfully under that floor, the sprint isn't the right fit yet. We'll say so on the call.
  • KPI Cost-per-qualified-lead or cost-per-customer. Your number, agreed in writing on the kickoff call, before any campaigns launch.
  • If we miss You owe nothing. The page rebuild, the VSL, the ad creative, the campaign structure, the data: all yours. We walk away. No claw-back, no consolation invoice.
  • If we hit You pick Path A or Path B. The post-sprint conversation is yours to lead. We don't enroll you into anything by default.

After the sprint

Two ways to keep going. Pick the one that fits.

If the sprint hits the KPI, you have a working acquisition system in your hands. The next question is who runs it. There are exactly two answers, and we've priced both.

Path A

Handoff. We finalize, you operate.

~$10K to $15K flat fee

For operators who want to bring media buying in-house once it's working. We finalize the campaigns, document the structure, and train the person on your team who will run it. Then we walk.

  • Final-pass campaign cleanup and structure documentation
  • Training session with your in-house operator or contractor
  • Creative library and asset handoff
  • One follow-up call within thirty days for questions
  • No retainer, no recurring fee

Path B

Performance partnership. We keep running it.

Negotiated per client

For operators who want the people who built it to keep operating it. The structure depends on what you sell, what your numbers look like, and where the math points. Always aligned to outcomes. No retainer without proof.

  • Ongoing media buying and creative iteration
  • Compensation tied to the KPI, not to hours
  • Quarterly review built into the agreement
  • Either side can exit on a defined window, no surprises
  • Terms agreed in writing on the post-sprint call

Honest fit check

Who the sprint is built for.

The structure works when the offer already converts and the bottleneck is qualified inbound volume. It does not work when there's no offer yet, or when the math on the product can't carry paid acquisition.

Built for: Operators selling something high-ticket. Average customer worth more than $2K, often much more. Offer already converts when the right person finds it. $1.5K to $3K of real ad budget to put behind it. No interest in learning media buying personally.

Not built for: Low-ticket products or subscriptions where the math doesn't carry paid acquisition. Pre-product founders who don't have an offer yet. Operators looking for a strategy deck. We can't sprint a hypothesis.

One call to see if your numbers fit.

Roughly forty-five minutes. We talk through the offer, the math, and the KPI you'd want on paper. If the sprint isn't the right structure for you, we'll tell you on the call.
